Censorship is a real problem. One major showcase of this is the county Eritrea, where the government shut down all public media in 2001 and with at least 16 journalists behind bars as of December 1, 2018. Other sources of information are restricted through the occasional signal jam, and overall slow internet. This is not only a problem in Eritrea, this problem is around the entire world.
